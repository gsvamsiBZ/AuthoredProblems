PROBLEM STATEMENT	
You are given an 8Ã—8 chessboard with your enemy king denoted by "K" and your queen denoted by "Q" rest all are empty cells denoted by ".".

INPUT FORMAT

OUTPUT FORMAT	
For each test case, print Yes if you can attack the king with your queen in the next move else print No.

CONSTRAINTS
